The annual MPS Family Picnic will take place on Friday June 19 between 6:00-8:00pm. This event is organized by your MPS School Council and relies on parents like you volunteering on the day of the event. Please consider volunteering for the shifts indicated below. This event would not be possible without your help.

Instructions for filling out the form:

  • Check all the shift that you are interested in.
  • If several family member wish to volunteer, have each person submit a separate form.
  • Please respond by Friday June 12.

School council will confirm which shifts you have been assigned to.

On behalf of the School Council, the staff and the students at MPS; Thank You for your support!
