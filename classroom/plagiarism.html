Plagiarism policy

Plagiarism is a serious academic offence and is an unacceptable practice in professional life and at all levels of studies. The following are the consequences for a student caught the first time plagiarizing work for a hand-in assignment:

  1. A zero mark on the assignment and 24hrs to resubmitt a new assignment.
  2. A note or phone call to parents.
  3. The new assignment will be marked out of a 50% of the original maximum score, e.g. if the plagirized assignment was out of 10 points the mark on the resubmitted assignment will be out 5 points.
Note that these consequences are only for first time offenders.
