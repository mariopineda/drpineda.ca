--- layout: page permalink: /outreach/index.html title: Outreach description: tags: image: feature: --- I am currently involved in the following outreach activities in science and engineering:

ESON

Edmonton Science Outreach Network (ESON) Scientists and Engineers-in-the-Classroom program. Through ESON I am providing grade 7 in-class presentations on mountain pine beetles. This presentation is aligned with the Alberta Program of Study for the Trees and Forests unit. If you want me to come to your classroom you can make a request through ESON.

FIRST Jr.FLL

FIRST Jr.FLL coaching of the Allendale LEGO Kids. If you are between 6-9, live in the Allendale community in Edmonton and are interested in becoming a LEGO ninja you may want to consider joining us.

ESON

Judging of the Edmonton Regional Science Fair.
