I mentor Opcom (Team 5064), a FIRST Robotics Competition team based in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada. The goals of Opcom is to continuously strive to actively improve ourselves and others by practising and promoting the Core Values of FIRST both in and out of competition..., oh, and we build awesome robots too. We work year round on improving out technical skills, team building, and promote personal development. Our team's motto is Go F.A.S.T. (Fast, Agile, Strong, Trained). We strive towards going F.A.S.T. continuously challenging ourselves to reach our and our team's full potential. We do not settle for easy solutions, we strive for F.A.S.T solutions.

We are recruiting team members, students and mentors, right now. See Team page for more information.
