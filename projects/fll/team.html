Who are we?

We are the The STEAMpunks - an Edmonton-based rookie FLL team. We are currently recruiting team members for the 2014 FLL season WORLD CLASS.

What's with the name, The STEAMpunks?

Steampunk is a sub-genre of science fiction that typically features steam-powered machinery, especially in a setting inspired by industrialised Western civilisation during the 19th century. By a similar token, The STEAMpunks (with STEAM in caps) focuses on building future technology and engineering solutions featuring 21st century technology and science. Our team's approach embraces the excitement of hands-on learning using Science, Technology, Engineering, Art and Mathematics - aka STEAM - to build tomorrow's solutions for today's challenges. We also happen to be huge fans of Jules Verne, the father of science fiction whose work epitomizes the awesomeness of steampunk.

We are The STEAMpunks - Building tomorrow's robots today!
