Do you want to support tomorrow's inventors, tinkerers, engineers and scientists today? The STEAMpunks are currently looking for sponsors for our 2014 competition season WORLD CLASS.

If you are interested in supporting us please contact our Lead Coach Dr. Pineda.

Why sponsoring The STEAMpunks?

Kids who participate in FIRST programs are:

  • 50 percent more likely to attend college
  • Twice as likely to major in science or engineering
  • Three times as likely to major specifically in engineering
  • Young women in FIRST programs are four times more likely to pursue science and engineering in college
  • Minority members of FIRST teams are more than twice as likely to enter the fields of science and engineering
Source: Brandeis University study

Budget

The cost to run a FLL team is relatively modest, with several of the high-ticket items being one time costs. Specifically, once a team has a LEGO robotics kit (a Mindstorms kit), the field table and the necessary computer hardware (typically a laptop) these can be reused from year to year. As a rookie team are looking for Founding Sponsors for these items. Sponsors that will provide support (cash or in-kind) for reusable items (as indicated in the table below) will be acknowledged as Founding Sponsors on our web site, t-shirts and promotional material every year that The STEAMpunks participate in the FLL program.

Typical FLL team expenses:

FLL Team registration$225
Robot kit (reusable)$400
FLL field set up kit$100
Computer hardware (reusable)$500
Tournament registration$175
Portable field table (reusable)$100
Promotional material (swag, buttons, banner, team t-shirts, etc.)$400
Total$1900

What can we do for you?

Support from sponsors is crucial to our success and we are deeply appreciative to all our sponsors for supporting science, engineering, and technology in children and youth. Your help in covering the costs of this program will allow the participation of children and youth independent of their financial situation.

There are four different levels of sponsorship but sponsors are not restricted to these levels. These are suggestions for anyone seeking to contribute. Sponsors may also choose to sponsor specific items outlined in the budget breakdown above. Any and all contributions are much appreciated.
