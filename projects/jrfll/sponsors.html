Sponsors

Our sponsors are an essential piece of our success. They provide the capacity that allows us to thrive. We're always thankful for past and present sponsors.

The Allendale Community League generously sponsored the Allendale LEGO Kids JrFLL team for the 2013 Super Seniors season and for the 2014 Disaster Blaster season.

Progressive Academy generously sponsored the Progressive Academy LEGO Ninjago JrFLL team for the 2014 Disaster Blaster season.
