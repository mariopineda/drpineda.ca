If you have any concerns about this policy, or feel that MPK Analytics is not abiding by this policy, please write or send an e-mail message to info@mpkanalytics.ca Your concerns will be addressed in a timely manner.

Information Collected and Used

MPK Analytics collects and uses your information only for the following purposes:
  • to provide you with our newsletter service;
  • to respond to your requests for further information about our analytics solutions;
  • to inform you of MPK Analytics programs, products or services that may be of interest to you; and
  • to meet legal and regulatory requirements.

Information That Does Not Identify You

MPK Analytics collects certain technical information from your computer’s Web browser when you visit www.mpkanalytics.com on the Internet. This information includes the amount of time spent on the site by a visitor with a unique Internet identifier, the pages visited and returned to, the operating system and Web browser used, and the referrer website. These server logs do not contain any personal information and can only be accessed by MPK Analytics. Server log files are used solely to track visits to our website and improve the content and layout of our site. MPK Analytics makes no attempt to link this traffic information to the individuals visiting our website.

Disclosure and Retention of Your Information

The personal information you provide to us through our site is never disclosed to any third party, without your explicit consent. We do not sell, trade or share any of your personal information with any other entities. However, we will release personal information when we believe such release is appropriate in order to comply with the law, for example, if we receive a subpoena or court order. When you send us a query or request for information, identifiable information about you is only kept long enough for us to provide you with a response. If you have subscribed to MPK Analytics’ newsletter, your e-mail address is kept as long as you are a subscriber. In the event that you choose to unsubscribe to MPK Analytics, your information will be immediately deleted from our database, and you will no longer receive any information from MPK Analytics. However, residual information about you may remain in back-up files temporarily.

Accuracy of the Information and Access to It

MPK Analytics relies exclusively on the information provided by its website users and subscribers, and will not seek independent verification of any of the information supplied. Thus, a newsletter subscriber or someone awaiting a response to a request for information is responsible for letting MPK Analytics know if their e-mail address has changed. By providing you with this Privacy Policy, MPK Analytics is pledging its continued commitment to protecting the information we collect. We regularly review our information-handling practices to ensure that we are abiding by this policy.
