--- layout: mathbio-page permalink: /index.html title: Introduction description: Applications of calculus to biological sciences tags: image: feature: /resources/mathbio/img/feature-mathbio.jpg --- This classroom resource was published as part of an assignment in EDSE 338 in the Faculty of Education at the University of Alberta.

This series of lessons is intended for students following the Alberta high school Program of Studies in Mathematics 31 and are taking the applications of calculus to biological sciences elective component. These lessons focus on the application of continuous time ordinary differential equations for modelling infectious disease dynamics using epidemiological compartment models.

Use the navigator bar to view the individual lessons (suitable for students) and the Teacher Page for more details on the lesson plans, prerequisites and learner outcomes.
