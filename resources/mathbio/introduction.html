Thinking about mathematical biology

Have students watch these video clips and answer the following focus questions in a class discussion.

Focus questions for the Math in the Wild video
  1. How can mathematical models be used in ecology and how can it be applied to environmental questions?
  2. How can mathematical models help us understand animal movement? Why is this important?
Focus questions for Zombie video
  1. Give specific examples of how can mathematics help us understand the dynamics of infectious diseases?
  2. What purpose do toy models, e.g. the zombie model and the Bieber fever model, play in increasing our understanding of the dynamics of infectious diseases? Give specific examples.
  3. How is the dynamics of moder day diseases, e.g. new strains of influenza, different from dynamics of infectious diseases in that plagued humans long time ago, e.g. the plague during the medieval times?
Focus questions for bee disease video
  1. What is the purpose of the mathematical modelling of the foul disease in bees?
  2. How can a mathematical model be used to manage a disease?
Focus questions for Math Modeling & Disease Dynamics video
  1. How can mathematical models help researchers in biology and medicine?
  2. What is the benefit of mathematical models compared to experiments?
