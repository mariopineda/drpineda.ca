--- layout: mathbio-page permalink: title: Introduction description: Applications of calculus to biological sciences tags: image: feature: /resources/mathbio/img/feature-mathbio.jpg --- This series of lessons is designed for students following the Alberta highschool Program of Studies in Mathematics 31 and are taking the applications of calculus to biological sciences elective component. These lessons focus on the application of continuous time ordinary differential equations for modelling infectious disease dynamics by using compartment models.
