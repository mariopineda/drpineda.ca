Subject
Physics
Objective
To build a self-supporting bridge using only notched pieces of wood.
Material
  • bridge dowel set with 2/3 notched dowels and 1/3 not notched dowels (see instructions for how to prepare the kit)
Instructions
  1. Take two dowels without notches and lay them out like this.
  2. Then take two pieces with notches and lay over the top.
  3. Then take another piece with no notches and lay over the top.
  4. This next stage is a little more difficult, but with a little care can be done rather easily. First tip; make sure you keep the pieces of dowel close, so you don’t have to stretch for pieces of the bridge. For this bit you need to take one of the outside dowels with no notches and left the bridge, and insert two notched dowels under the outside dowel and on top of the centre dowel.
Think about
How does it work?
It requires no nails or ropes to hold it together – the bridge is self-supporting.
