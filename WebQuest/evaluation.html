You will be evaluated on two portions of this assignment, the poster presentation and a report. Your poster (and the poster presentation to your peers) is worth 50% of your mark and your individual report is worth 50% of your mark.

The following rubrics will be used to evaluate your poster and report. Please use these as a guide when creating your poster and report.

Rubric for evaluating posters

Categories Needs Improvement (1) Sufficient (2) Proficient (3) Excellent (4) Total marks
Knowledge Group does not have grasp of information; questions cannot be answered Group is uncomfortable with information and is only able to answer rudimentary questions Group is at ease with content, but fails to elaborate Group demonstrates full knowledge (more than required) with explanations and elaboration ___ / 4
Terminology Too many unexplained technical terms; lack of proper language throughout Some technical terms explained; some proper language used Most technical terms explained; proper language used predominantly Technical terms explained in the correct context; proper language used throughout ___ / 4
Display/Presentation The poster was incomplete or chaotic with no clear plan; many labels were missing or incorrect; mumbling presentation too quiet to hear Some element on the poster had a function and clearly served to illustrate some aspect of the research findings; Some items, graphs etc. were correctly labeled; unclear presentation with many stumbles Each element on the poster had a function and clearly served to illustrate some aspect of the research findings; most items, graphs etc. were neatly and correctly labeled; clear presentation with a few stumbles Each element on the poster had a function and clearly served to illustrate some aspect of the research findings; all items, graphs etc. were neatly and correctly labeled; no stumbles and clear voice during presentation ___ / 4
Required Elements Several required elements were missing All but 1 of the required elements were included on the poster All required elements were included on the poster The poster included all required elements as well as additional information ___ / 4
Quality of Information Information had little or nothing to do with the main topic Information clearly related to the main topic; no details and/or examples were given Information clearly related to the main topic; provided 1-2 supporting details and/or examples Information clearly related to the main topic; included several supporting details and/or examples ___ / 4
Spelling/grammar Lots of spelling and grammar mistakes A few mistakes Fewer than 5 mistakes No mistakes ___ / 4
