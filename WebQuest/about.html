--- layout: webquest-page permalink: /about.html title: About description: tags: image: feature: /WebQuest/img/tarsands.jpg --- In this WebQuest students learn about the significance of Canada's withdrawal from the Kyoto Protocol by researching the many ways in which antrophogenic climate change is affecting our world. This WebQuest was developed as part of an assignment for EDSE 452 during the fall 2013 semester at the Faculty of Education, University of Alberta.
